Vivek Sinha¶

Background¶

The Toronto Parking Authority manages the Bike Share Toronto program, a form of local mass transit and an enjoyable way to travel the city by bicycle. The Bike Share Toronto system consists of multiple bicycles, stations and docking points. It keeps track of all the users hiring the bike, the source station from where the bike was hired, the destination station where the bike was docked, the duration of the trip, and the start and end time of the trip, among others. Overage fees can be avoided if the user docks the bike within 30 min of its usage, and then hires another bike to reach the destination. The dataset was taken from the kaggle website, for which the link is given below:

https://www.kaggle.com/jackywang529/toronto-bikeshare-data

The datasets used, however, are only for the year 2018.

Goal of the Analysis¶

The motive of this analysis is to extract vital information about how the user experiance can be improved based on the data collected over a period of time. Some of the business questions to be answered are as follows:

  1. The common stations traversed by the users over the entire year
  2. Most frequently used source and destination stations every quarter
  3. The number of bikes hired every month/ every hour/ every quarter
  4. The time of the day most of the hirings happen
  5. The total users who have paid the overage fees within the year

def combineDF(df1, df2, df3, df4):
    df1 = df1.union(df2)
    df1 = df1.union(df3)
    df1 = df1.union(df4)
    return df1

bikeDF = combineDF(bike_Q1, bike_Q2, bike_Q3, bike_Q4)

timestamp_time_of_day = timestamp_data.withColumn("time_of_day",\
    f.when((f.col("start_hour") == 0) | (f.col("start_hour") == 1) | \
           (f.col("start_hour") == 2) | (f.col("start_hour") == 3),"midnight: 0-3")\
    .when((f.col("start_hour") == 4) | (f.col("start_hour") == 5) | \
          (f.col("start_hour") == 6) | (f.col("start_hour") == 7),"early morning: 4-7")\
    .when((f.col("start_hour") == 8) | (f.col("start_hour") == 9) | \
          (f.col("start_hour") == 10) | (f.col("start_hour") == 11),"morning: 8-11")\
    .when((f.col("start_hour") == 12) | (f.col("start_hour") == 13) | \
          (f.col("start_hour") == 14) | (f.col("start_hour") == 15),"noon: 12-15")\
    .when((f.col("start_hour") == 16) | (f.col("start_hour") == 17) | \
          (f.col("start_hour") == 18) | (f.col("start_hour") == 19),"evening: 16-19")\

          (f.col("start_hour") == 22) | (f.col("start_hour") == 23),"night: 20-13")\
